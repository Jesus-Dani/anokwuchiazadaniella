Clarity Sessions

A Clarity Session isn't a one-off call — it's a full program that walks with you through execution, not just a single conversation. Built around whatever idea, decision, or goal you've been sitting with but can't quite move on, I use a six-stage framework I developed called SCULPT to work through it properly — starting with who you actually are, not the version you present, through to where this fits into everything else you're building. And it doesn't stop at clarity — I track and support you through the different stages of actually executing it. You don't leave with a feeling. You leave with a plan, and someone checking in as you move through it.
