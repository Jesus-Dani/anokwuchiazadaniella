Meet and Shop

A pop-up market series I run for student entrepreneurs to sell directly to their campus community.

Meet and Shop is a pop-up market I've run three times over two years — each one turning a stretch of campus into a real marketplace for student entrepreneurs.

Across the three sessions, 120+ student entrepreneurs have sold directly to their own campus community — no middleman, just a table and a reason to show up.

We've also shown up for Redeemer's University Student Association Tradefair Week three years running — 18 days total of vendors selling side by side.

People come to browse and end up staying — trying things on, comparing prices, actually talking to the person who made or sourced what they're buying.

Altogether, these markets have moved over ₦100M in student vendor sales. That's not pocket money — that's real proof student businesses can work.
