Kingdom Wealth Forum

A conference exploring faith, purpose, and financial intentionality — hosted at Redeemer's University.

Kingdom Wealth Forum 1.0 — Equipped for Life. I convened this to get real about the skills required to actually thrive, not just survive, with speakers from business, academia, and ministry all weighing in.

Hosting it alongside people who cared about the same questions I did made the whole thing feel less like an event and more like a real conversation.

Every speaker circled back to the same question: what's the actual impact of the choices you're making right now? That's the kind of pressure-testing I wanted people to leave with.

The turnout told me something — this wasn't just a question I was quietly sitting with. A lot of us are.

People stayed. People took notes, took photos, asked questions after everyone else had left. That's the whole point — not just attending, but actually taking something with you.
